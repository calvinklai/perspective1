This task is interested in how people construct life details from visual information.

In a moment, you will have the opportunity to write a brief essay about a person you've never met. The particular person you’ll be writing about will be determined on a random basis.

On the screen that follows, you will be asked to select one of 8 boxes. Each box corresponds to a potential individual. On making your selection, you will see a photo of the person, along with specific instructions for writing your essay.

Because we are interested in how people construct life event details from visual information alone, you will not be provided with any additional information about this person beyond what’s contained in the photo.

Click ‘Continue’ to make your selection.
